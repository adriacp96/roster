Easy Roster Copy (Bookmarklet)

Use this bookmark to **instantly copy your roster text** without manual selection. This is the easiest and most reliable way, especially on iPhone.

Setup Instructions

  1. On Computer: Drag this link to your bookmarks bar: Copy Roster
  2. On iPhone/iPad:
    • Bookmark this page.
    • Edit the new bookmark, rename it to `Copy Roster`.
    • Clear the address and paste the code from the box below.

Process Your Roster

Go to the roster page, click your "Copy Roster" bookmark, then come back here and paste into the box below.
